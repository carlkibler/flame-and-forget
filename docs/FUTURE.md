# Future Feature Ideas for Flame and Forget

This document captures potential enhancements and features to consider for future development.

## 🎨 Visual/Aesthetic

- **Sound effects** - Subtle whoosh when card flies, crackle when it burns ✅ (implemented)
- **Particle effects** - Embers floating up from the fire as cards burn
- **Different burn animations** - Some cards could crumble, some dissolve, some turn to ash
- **Customizable fire color** - Blue flames, green flames, rainbow flames
- **Night/day mode** - Different background gradients (dawn, dusk, midnight)
- **Seasonal themes** - Falling leaves in autumn, snow in winter, fireflies in summer ✅ (implemented)

## 🎮 Interaction/Gameplay

- **Drag and drop** - Physically drag cards into the fire instead of auto-burn
- **Combo counter** - Burn multiple things quickly for "letting go streak!"
- **Satisfying physics** - Cards bounce off each other in the queue
- **Hold to burn longer** - Press and hold on a card to watch it burn more dramatically
- **Shake to clear** - Shake device to burn everything at once (mobile)

## 📊 Tracking/Reflection

- **Stats page** - "You've let go of 247 things this year"
- **Word cloud** - Most common words/themes you've burned
- **Time of day patterns** - Graph showing when you use it most
- **Mood tracking** - Quick emoji before/after burning
- **Streak tracking** - "7 days of letting go"

## 🎯 Content Features

- **Prompt generator** - Suggest things to let go of if user is stuck
- **Categories** - Tag as "regret", "cringe", "anger", "worry" with different colored cards
- **Quick templates** - "I forgive myself for...", "I'm done worrying about..."
- **Voice input** - Speak what you want to burn
- **Photo burning** - Upload/take a photo to symbolically burn

## 🤝 Social (Optional)

- **Anonymous sharing** - See what others have burned (sanitized/aggregated)
- **Shared bonfires** - Group session where friends burn things together
- **Support messages** - After burning, get a random encouraging message

## 🛠️ Practical

- **Export history** - Download your history as JSON/CSV
- **Scheduled burns** - Automatically burn certain recurring thoughts
- **Dark secrets mode** - Extra secure, nothing saved to history
- **Ritual timer** - "Take 3 deep breaths before burning"
- **Undo last burn** - "Actually, I need to process that more"

## 🎭 Whimsical

- **Achievement badges** - "First burn", "100 burns", "Midnight burner"
- **Card styles** - Post-it notes, scraps of paper, stone tablets
- **Ceremonial mode** - More dramatic staging, candles appear, dramatic music
- **Fortune cookie** - After burning, get a random wisdom quote
- **Pet/mascot** - A little creature that cheers when you burn things

## Priority Quick Wins

The most impactful features that would be relatively straightforward to implement:

1. **Sound effects** ✅ - Adds immersion without complexity
2. **Drag-and-drop** - More satisfying interaction
3. **Prompt generator** - Helps users who are stuck
4. **Particle effects** - Visual polish that enhances the burn experience
5. **Stats page** - Simple count/history visualization
